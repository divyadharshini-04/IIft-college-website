About IIFT

A premier destination for students aspiring to excel in fashion design, apparel technology, textile innovation, and creative leadership.

Redefining Fashion Education

At IIFT, we blend creativity, craftsmanship, and technology to prepare students for the evolving global fashion industry. Our programs focus on design thinking, practical exposure, sustainability, and entrepreneurship.

With expert faculty, modern labs, industry partnerships, and innovation-led learning, we nurture the next generation of fashion professionals.

Programs Offered

Industry-oriented academic programs designed to build creative, technical, and managerial capabilities.

Fashion Design

Comprehensive training in design fundamentals, garment construction, trend research, and collection development.

Apparel Production

Learn manufacturing systems, quality control, merchandising, and production planning for the apparel sector.

Textile Design

Develop expertise in fabric innovation, print development, surface ornamentation, and textile applications.

Fashion Communication

Build skills in branding, styling, digital media, visual communication, and fashion marketing strategy.
