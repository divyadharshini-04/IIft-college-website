About IIFT

Empowering Creativity Through Fashion, Innovation & Technology

Who We Are

Leading Fashion Education Since 2001

Indian Institute of Fashion Technology (IIFT) is committed to providing world-class education in fashion design, textile innovation, apparel production and fashion communication.

We combine creativity, technology and industry exposure to develop professionals who can excel in the global fashion and lifestyle industry.

Our students gain practical experience through workshops, internships, industry collaborations and live projects.

At IIFT, learning extends beyond the classroom through fashion shows, industry visits, design exhibitions, and collaborative projects with leading fashion brands. Students are encouraged to explore their creativity while developing practical skills that meet industry demands.

Our state-of-the-art design studios, textile laboratories, and digital fashion technology facilities provide an inspiring environment for innovation and experimentation. We focus on nurturing confidence, leadership, and entrepreneurial thinking among our students.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To nurture creative talent, promote innovation, and prepare students for successful careers in fashion, design and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized institution known for excellence in fashion education, research and industry collaboration.

Core Values

Creativity, Sustainability, Professionalism, Innovation, Leadership and Lifelong Learning.
