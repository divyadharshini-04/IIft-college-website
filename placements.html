Placements & Career Development

At IIFT, we believe that education is successful only when it leads to meaningful career opportunities. Our dedicated Placement and Career Development Cell works closely with students and industry partners to ensure every graduate is prepared for the professional world.

Placement Overview

The Placement Cell at the Indian Institute of Fashion Technology bridges the gap between academic learning and industry expectations. Through training programs, internships, workshops, portfolio development sessions, mock interviews and recruitment drives, we prepare students to excel in the competitive fashion and lifestyle industry.

Over the years, IIFT has built strong relationships with leading fashion brands, textile companies, retail organizations, design houses and creative agencies. Our graduates have successfully secured positions in design, merchandising, marketing, branding, production management and entrepreneurship.
