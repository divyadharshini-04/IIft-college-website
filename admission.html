Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to identify talented and passionate students who aspire to build a successful career in the fashion industry.

Step 1

Choose your preferred program and submit an application.

Step 2

Upload academic documents and personal details.

Step 3

Attend counselling/interview if required.

Step 4

Receive admission confirmation and pay fees.

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • 10+2 from a recognized board
  • Minimum qualifying marks as prescribed
  • Passion for design and creativity

Diploma Programs

  • 10th or 12th Pass
  • No prior design experience required
  • Open to all streams
